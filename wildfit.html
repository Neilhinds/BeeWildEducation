WildFit

Improve your physical fitness and mental wellbeing with my Wildfit program, designed to engage your muscles, lungs and mind through active movement, mindfulness activities and forest yoga.

WildFit is an English-language movement and wellbeing project designed for primary and middle (media) schools. Delivered entirely in English by a mother-tongue English teacher, WildFit combines physical activity, outdoor learning and play-based education to support children’s mental, emotional and physical development

Through structured games, cooperative challenges and nature-based activities, students develop coordination, balance, strength and overall fitness, while also strengthening key life skills such as teamwork, communication, problem-solving and resilience. Activities are adapted to each age group and ability level, ensuring an inclusive and motivating experience for all participants.

WildFit predominantly takes place in outdoor spaces and natural environments—school gardens, parks, green areas and open spaces—encouraging children to reconnect with nature while moving, playing and learning in a dynamic, low-pressure setting. English is used naturally and practically throughout each session, allowing students to improve listening, comprehension and spoken communication without the constraints of a traditional classroom.

By combining movement, language and nature, WildFit promotes healthy habits, boosts confidence, reduces stress and supports overall wellbeing—helping children learn not only how to move better, but how to feel better too.
